Weapons

Sabre

Sabre is faced paced and requires agility, speed and a quick reaction time. Sabre uses the concept of priority to judge who gets the point

Epee

Epee is a slower paced game needing strategy and paitence. All you need to do is to try hit the other person without getting hit.

Foil

Foil is a popular weapon that requires precision and agility. Foil uses the concept of priority to judge who gets the point
